Post-Match Presentation

Axar Patel · Player of the Match

"It's the perfect start to the series. The T20 series didn't go well, so I'm very happy with the way I performed today. I thought I needed to bowl in the right areas, keep it tight, and attack the stumps. When I came in, Sundar was already at the crease. We had lost a couple of wickets, so we knew we had to build a partnership first."

Shubman Gill · India Captain

"Definitely, it's the perfect start. When you see your middle order and lower order scoring runs and finishing the game for the team, it gives you a lot of confidence as a captain. I was really pleased with the way we bowled in the middle overs — taking those five wickets was outstanding."

Harry Brook · England Captain

"Um, not ideal. Obviously we lost 5 wickets for 20 in that middle period which would obviously put us on the back foot. We thought we could defend that score. It was a great effort from the lads."
